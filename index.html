Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding ordering, custom design times, and hand-delivery details.

You can order directly from this website! Simply click the "Order Now" button on any cake card to customize your size, message, and eggless option, then instantly submit via WhatsApp. For completely custom architectural cakes, you can also fill out the contact form below or call our Madison Avenue studio.

We deliver throughout Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens between 9:00 AM and 7:00 PM daily. You can choose a preferred 2-hour delivery slot during order checkout. Standard delivery fees apply based on distance.

For custom birthday and anniversary cakes, we recommend ordering 3 to 5 days in advance. For large-scale wedding cakes, we recommend booking a tasting and finalizing the design at least 2 to 4 weeks prior to the ceremony date.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. For orders placed via our WhatsApp system, we will email you a secure digital invoice link for quick payment.

Yes! We have a select menu of our best selling cakes and pastries available for same-day delivery. Please place your order before 10:00 AM to guarantee delivery on the same afternoon.

Absolutely! Almost all of our signature cake cake bases, including our dark chocolate truffle and crimson velvet cakes, can be prepared 100% eggless. Simply toggle the "Eggless" choice on the order modal.

For standard menu orders, cancellations are accepted up to 48 hours before the scheduled delivery for a full refund. For custom and wedding cakes, cancellations must be made 7 days prior; otherwise, the 50% deposit is retained to cover specialty design assets.
